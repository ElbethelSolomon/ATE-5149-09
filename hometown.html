GET TO ADDIS ABABA

Addis Ababa is a highly developed and important cultural, artistic, financial and administrative centre of Ethiopia. Several international organizations have their headquarters there, including the Organization of African Unity. Addis Ababa is known for its vibrant jazz scene, with numerous jazz clubs and festivals featuring talented local and international musicians.
